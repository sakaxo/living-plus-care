DOMICILIARY CARE SERVICE

At Living Plus Care Services, we understand that independence is a core aspect of maintaining your wellbeing and quality of life. That's why we offer top-tier domiciliary care services, designed to support your ability to live comfortably, safely, and independently in your own home.


What Is Domiciliary Care?

Domiciliary care, also known as home care, is a service that supports individuals in their own homes with activities of daily living. Our care services are personalised, flexible, and planned according to your individual needs and lifestyle preferences. Whether you need help with daily tasks, personal care, or specialist care related to specific conditions, our trained caregivers are here to support you.

Our Domiciliary Care Services


  • Personal Care

    We offer support with personal care tasks such as bathing, dressing, toileting, and grooming. Our caregivers respect your privacy and dignity while ensuring your personal hygiene needs are met.

  • Companionship

    Loneliness can be challenging, but our caregivers offer friendly companionship, engaging in conversations, hobbies, or outings, helping to enrich your life with meaningful interactions.

  • Night Care

    We provide overnight care services to ensure your safety and well-being throughout the night. Whether you need assistance with bedtime routines, medication, or reassurance during the night, our caregivers are there for you.

  • Live-In Care

    Our live-in care service provides round-the-clock support, ensuring you receive the help you need whenever you need it. This is a perfect solution for those needing continuous care and wish to remain at home.

  • Catheter Care

    Our team is trained in managing and providing care for individuals with catheters, ensuring proper hygiene and monitoring to prevent infections.

  • Meal Preparation and Diet Support

    Proper nutrition is essential for health and wellbeing. Our caregivers can assist with meal preparation, following dietary guidelines, and ensuring you enjoy a nutritious diet.

  • Household Tasks

    Keeping your home clean and organised can enhance your quality of life. Our caregivers can help with light housekeeping tasks like laundry, dusting, vacuuming, and more.

  • Stoma Care

    Living with a stoma can be challenging, but our caregivers can help manage your stoma care routine, giving you peace of mind and promoting your independence.

  • Medication Assistance

    We can support with medication administration, ensuring you take the right medicine at the right time, contributing to your overall health and wellbeing.


At Living Plus Care Services, we believe in delivering care that improves lives, supports independence, and ensures our clients can continue to live at home where they are most comfortable. Our domiciliary care services are more than just help - they are a commitment to enhancing your lifestyle and well-being. Why Opt for Domiciliary Care?
Benefits / Advantages of Domiciliary Care

Choosing domiciliary care can provide numerous benefits that directly enhance the quality of Life for you or your Loved ones. Here are some reasons why you might consider this type of care:


  • Comfort of Your Own Home

    There's no place Like home. Domiciliary care allows individuals to stay in their familiar and comforting environment. Being surrounded by personal belongings and cherished memories can greatly enhance their sense of well-being and independence.

  • Personalised Care

    Unlike residential care, domiciliary care offers a highly personalised service, tailored to the unique needs and routines of each individual. This means the care you receive is always designed around your specific requirements, lifestyle, and preferences.

  • Maintain Independence

    One of the primary goals of domiciliary care is to promote independence. Our caregivers support you with daily tasks and personal care, but also encourage you to do as much as you can for yourself, helping to maintain your independence and self-esteem.

  • Flexible and Adaptable

    Domiciliary care services can be adapted to evolving needs. Whether you need a few hours of support each week or round-the-clock live-in care, services can be adjusted to ensure you receive the right Level of care at the right time.

  • Companionship and Emotional Support

    Domiciliary care is not just about practical support. Our caregivers also provide companionship and emotional support, helping to combat feelings of loneliness and isolation.

  • Peace of Mind for Loved Ones

    For family members, knowing that their loved one is receiving professional care in the comfort of their own home can bring immense peace of mind. You can rest assured that your loved one's needs are being met in a caring and respectful manner.

  • Cost-Effective Solution

    Compared to residential care, domiciliary care can be a more cost-effective solution. You pay for the care you need, which can be adapted and scaled up or down as requirements change.

  • Supports Physical Health

    Besides assisting with personal care and daily tasks, our caregivers can also aid in the administration of medication, nutrition planning, and exercise routines, helping maintain optimal physical health.

  • Helps with Housekeeping

    Our care providers can assist with light household chores such as cleaning, laundry, and meal preparation, ensuring a clean, organised, and comfortable living environment.

  • Continuity of Care

    With domiciliary care, a strong relationship is developed between the caregiver and the client. This continuity of care ensures that the caregiver knows the client's needs and preferences intimately, Leading to a higher standard of care.

  • Retain Local Connections

    Staying at home means maintaining Links with friends, neighbours, and the local community. This social connection plays a vital role in mental and emotional wellbeing.

  • Pet-Friendly

    Pets often provide significant companionship and emotional support. With domiciliary care, you or your loved ones can continue to live with and care for pets.

  • Safeguards Family Relationships

    By alleviating the pressure of care, domiciliary care can help maintain healthy and positive family relationships. It enables family members to spend quality time with their Loved ones, instead of solely focusing on caregiving.

  • Less Disruptive

    For people living with dementia or other cognitive impairments, changing living environments can be highly disruptive and distressing. Domiciliary care minimises this disruption by providing care in the familiar setting of their own home.


Choosing domiciliary care with Living Plus Care Services guarantees high-quality care tailored to your specific needs, while also ensuring the preservation of personal freedom and familiarity of home. Feel free to contact us to explore more about our comprehensive domiciliary care offerings.

Who Needs Domiciliary Care?

Identifying the Need for Domiciliary Care: Is it Right For You?


Domiciliary care can be the answer for diverse individuals seeking assistance in their daily lives. It can be a lifeline for those who desire to maintain their independence while Living comfortably and securely at home. The following groups might consider domiciliary care:


  • Seniors Seeking Assistance

    As we age, certain tasks can become challenging. Elderly individuals who need support with daily chores, personal care, and mobility, or those combatting feelings of loneliness, can hugely benefit from our specialised domiciliary care services.

  • Persons with Long-Term Health Conditions

    Those Living with chronic illnesses such as cardiovascular disease, COPD, diabetes, or neurological conditions Like Parkinson's disease often necessitate regular, competent care and support that our expert caregivers can deliver.

  • Individuals with Physical Impairments

    We extend our care services to those Living with physical disabilities, offering aid with mobility, daily tasks, and personal care, helping them lead fulfilling lives.

  • Recovering Individuals Post Surgery or Illness

    If you're recovering from a major surgical procedure or recuperating from a severe illness, domiciliary care can be an ideal option. Our care services provide the necessary support as you journey back to health and regain your independence.

  • Persons Living With Dementia Or Alzheimer's

    Our trained caregivers offer empathetic, specialised care for individuals navigating life with dementia or Alzheimer's. We ensure their safety and comfort while promoting cognitive health through stimulating activities.

  • Adults in Need of Palliative Care

    For those requiring end-of-life care, our team provides sensitive, respectful support, always prioritising the comfort and dignity of the individual.


More Categories of Individuals Who Might Find Domiciliary Care Beneficial

  • Individuals with Mental Health Challenges

    People dealing with mental health conditions such as depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, or PTSD can immensely benefit from the supportive and understanding care provided by our trained caregivers. This assistance can facilitate management of their everyday tasks, help in crisis prevention, and lend emotional support.

  • Young Adults with Learning Disabilities

    Young adults who are navigating life with learning disabilities may find domiciliary care invaluable. We offer assistance with social engagement, skill development, and day-to-day tasks, fostering independence and confidence.

  • Individuals Requiring Respite Care

    For family caregivers in need of a short break or respite, our caregivers can step in to ensure continuity of care for your loved one. This service provides relief for the primary caregivers and ensures that the individual in need of care continues to receive the support they need.

  • Persons Dealing with Sensory Impairment

    For individuals experiencing loss of sight or hearing, our caregivers can provide assistance with communication, mobility, and everyday tasks. They can also offer guidance in using adaptive techniques and assistive devices.

  • Parents With New-Borns Or Multiples

    New parents, especially those with multiple infants, can benefit from an extra pair of hands. Our caregivers can assist with infant care, errands, meal preparation, and light housekeeping, allowing parents to focus on bonding with their new-borns.

  • Individuals Undergoing Cancer Treatment

    People undergoing rigorous treatment regimes for cancer often require extra support. Domiciliary care can provide assistance with medication management, transportation to medical appointments, meal preparation, and emotional support.

  • Individuals With Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD)

    For both children and adults on the autism spectrum, our caregivers can offer a supportive and understanding environment. They assist with communication, social interaction, routine establishment, and day-to-day tasks.

Each person's needs are unique, and we take pride in providing customised care plans that cater to your specific circumstances and preferences. Get in touch with us today to explore how our devoted team at Living Plus Care Services can support you or your loved ones in the journey of living comfortably at home.
