What Is Supported Living?

Supported living is a person-centred approach to providing care that focuses on enabling individuals to Live as independently as possible in their own homes, with tailored support to meet their unique needs and aspirations.

This service is designed for individuals who may require assistance due to disability, age, illness, or other Life circumstances, but who value their independence and wish to maintain it. With supported Living, individuals have the chance to Lead fulfilling Lives within their communities, making their own choices and decisions about how they Live, with a helping hand from skilled care professionals when needed.

Who Can Benefit From Supported Living?

1. Individuals with Learning Disabilities: Supported living is beneficial for those with Learning disabilities, assisting them to Lead fulfilling, autonomous Lives while still receiving the necessary support.

2. Autism Spectrum Disorders: Those on the autism spectrum can greatly benefit from supported living, as it helps them maintain routine, develop social skills, and promotes a sense of independence.

3. Mental Health Conditions: Individuals struggling with mental health conditions can utilise supported Living for a balanced Life, with ongoing support for managing their mental weLlbeing.

4. Physical Disabilities: Those with physical disabilities can receive necessary assistance while fostering independence in daily tasks, mobility, and social interaction.

5. Elderly Individuals Needing Support: Elderly individuals wishing to maintain their independence but requiring some Level of support can also opt for supported living services.

6. Individuals with Long-term Health Conditions: Those with chronic diseases or Long-term health conditions can maintain their health management and independence with the assistance of supported living.

7. Transition from Residential Care or Hospital: If an individual has been in residential. care or hospital. and is now ready to transition to more independent living, supported living can provide the ideal. step-down.
